Featured Adventures

Family Fun Rapids

Difficulty: Beginner Duration: 4 hours Price: $80/person

Perfect for families and first-time rafters! Navigate gentle Class I-II rapids while enjoying spectacular canyon scenery. Our expert guides ensure a safe and fun experience for all ages (8+). Includes lunch, safety equipment, and transportation from our base camp.

  • Family-friendly adventure
  • Professional guides included
  • All safety equipment provided
  • Scenic canyon views

Intermediate Canyon Run

Difficulty: Intermediate Duration: 6 hours Price: $120/person

Step up the excitement with Class III rapids and stunning wilderness scenery. This full-day adventure includes challenging rapids, swimming opportunities, and a riverside lunch. Perfect for those seeking more thrills while still being accessible to most fitness levels.

  • Class III rapids excitement
  • Full-day wilderness experience
  • Swimming and cliff jumping
  • Gourmet riverside lunch

Extreme Rapids Challenge

Difficulty: Expert Duration: 8 hours Price: $180/person

For experienced rafters only! Tackle Class IV-V rapids in some of the most challenging and remote sections of the river. This adrenaline-pumping adventure requires previous rafting experience and excellent physical fitness. Includes overnight camping option.

  • Class IV-V extreme rapids
  • Remote wilderness access
  • Overnight camping available
  • Expert guides only
